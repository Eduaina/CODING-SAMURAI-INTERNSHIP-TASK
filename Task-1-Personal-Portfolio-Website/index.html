About Me

Bridging Biochemistry & Digital Experiences: A User-Centric Frontend Developer.

As a Front-End Developer, I craft intuitive and robust digital experiences, driven by a deep understanding of user needs and a rigorous, analytical mindset. My unique background in Medical Biochemistry and research has cultivated a meticulous attention to detail and a scientific approach to problem-solving, which I now channel into building exceptional web interfaces.

I approach development with a strong product-thinking perspective, leveraging extensive UX research skills – from user stories and wireframing to comprehensive UX flows and problem framing – to ensure every line of code translates into genuine user value. Known for my tenacity, rapid learning, and insatiable curiosity, I am always seeking growth and innovative solutions. I am committed to delivering clean, scalable, and accessible front-end architecture, ensuring seamless performance across all devices.

Beyond coding, I am an aspiring technical writer, dedicated to clear documentation and enhancing developer experience. This holistic approach empowers me to not only build functional interfaces but to also articulate complex solutions with precision and clarity. I thrive on transforming intricate challenges into elegant, user-friendly solutions.
